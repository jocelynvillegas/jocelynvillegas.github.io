About Commissions

A commission price includes an hourly rate, a fixed fee for maintaining supplies, and a fixed fee for adjustments or changes to the artwork within reason. Please keep in mind that after the acceptance of a personalized invoice detailing the total price, I request half of the payment upfront before the completion of the piece.

Please see below for all of my hourly rates!

Digital Artwork

$25.00

Stylized artwork created digitally via Adobe Creative Cloud tools or Procreate. You will receive a digital file of the piece and/or a print.

Traditional Painting

$28.00

A hand-painted illustrative realism piece created with either watercolor, acrylic, or gouache paint.

Mural Work

$32.00

Stylized or illustrative realism art on an exterior or interior wall of a home or business. Hand-painted design either by original photo reference or created digitally by artist beforehand.
