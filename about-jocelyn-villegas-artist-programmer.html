I'm Jocelyn! I'm a Mexican American artist and computer science student. My creative work is inspired by Chicano pride, cultural identity, and the nature of the world we all share. I am currently working in IT for the Colorado State University library system, and I have enjoyed learning and gaining experience in system administration and configuration management.

Studying computer science offers many possibilities, and my ultimate career goal is to work in a job where I can be creative, problem-solve, and always keep learning. When I'm not studying, I typically spend my time reading novels, painting, or playing the Legend of Zelda.

As an artist, creative thinking is essential to how I problem-solve and view the world - and I want to always develop technological tools that are ethical and committed to humankind's best interests.
